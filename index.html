2.Original intention

Finance is a service industry in various fields. Business school is a stage to cultivate future business leaders. The financial club of Kaiyi Business School in France is committed to the seamless connection between finance and business, and promotes knowledge, talent, capital, public welfare and other fields. The cross-border integration helps to achieve information communication and business cooperation between alumni and alumni and the outside world, creating opportunities for mutual benefit and win-win for financial sector practitioners and related industry personnel.

3.Vision

Short-term tasks: Seminars, through the analysis of the development of macro, geography, industry and other economic situations, will contribute to the continuous learning and commercial layout of the post-business school era; professional learning seminars can also be implemented through the club platform to achieve Life-long Active advocacy of learning. Medium-term goals: Roadshows and docking capital help alumni improve their business model, and capital-assisted business growth, there is also a source of investment opportunities for capital. The club designates a special person to collect alumni entrepreneurship or business plans, explore FA talents among alumni, cooperate with external experts, capital, investors, assist alumni to improve business plans, and strive to achieve one or two roadshows each year to help alumni business take off. Long-term vision: Prepare and operate alumni foundations, let the business plug in the financial wings, and realize a beautiful vision for public welfare. There are many alumni who are charitable, able to make professional fundraising, give reasonable tax deductions to donated enterprises, effectively organize charitable activities, and effectively manage the stock assets. It is possible to realize the public welfare through the alumni foundation. More visions of field alumni, while contributing to the development of alumni activities.
